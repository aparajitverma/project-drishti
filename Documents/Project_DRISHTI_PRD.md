# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project DRISHTI: Digital Refinery Intelligence & Safety Holistic Tracking Initiative**

## 1. Introduction & Objective
Project DRISHTI is an AI-powered simulation and intelligence dashboard designed to transition modern oil refineries from reactive monitoring to predictive optimization. The objective is to maximize profit margins, ensure zero unplanned downtime, and strictly enforce ESG compliance by providing real-time, prescriptive operational intelligence.

## 2. Target Audience & User Roles
- **Refinery Executives / Plant Managers:** Require high-level financial impact metrics (e.g., net margin uplift, daily profit impact) and ESG compliance status.
- **Control Room Operators:** Need actionable, safe tuning recommendations, real-time alerts, and interactive simulation levers to test adjustments safely.
- **Maintenance Leads:** Require early warning systems (48-72 hours) on asset degradation to plan maintenance efficiently.

## 3. Core Product Features
### 3.1 Yield Optimization Cockpit
- **Feature:** Real-time prediction of Diesel and Petrol quality using soft sensors.
- **Value:** Prevents margin leakage caused by delayed lab results.
- **Interaction:** Operators can adjust Furnace Temperature, Column Pressure, and Reflux Ratio via interactive sliders to see the predicted impact on quality and profit.

### 3.2 Predictive Maintenance Center
- **Feature:** Machine learning-driven Remaining Useful Life (RUL) prediction for critical rotating equipment.
- **Value:** Shifts maintenance from schedule-based to condition-based, preventing catastrophic failures.
- **Interaction:** Visual degradation curves and simulated load adjustments to observe risk impacts.

### 3.3 Benzene Safety (Winterization Guard)
- **Feature:** Pipeline thermal profile tracking predicting freeze risks based on ambient conditions.
- **Value:** Avoids multi-million dollar pipeline blockages during winter cold snaps.
- **Interaction:** Dynamic steam-flow adjustment controls to mitigate predicted temperature drops.

### 3.4 Flare & ESG Tracker
- **Feature:** 30-minute advance forecasting of flare drum surges based on upstream pressure anomalies.
- **Value:** Avoids regulatory fines and recovers valuable hydrocarbon gas.
- **Interaction:** Compressor recycle valve adjustments to reroute gas and flatten the predicted flare surge.

## 4. User Stories
- *As a Plant Manager, I want to see the estimated daily financial uplift so I can quantify the ROI of the AI system.*
- *As a Control Room Operator, I want the system to recommend a specific furnace temperature adjustment so I can maximize yield without risking an off-spec product.*
- *As an ESG Compliance Officer, I want to be alerted 30 minutes before a flare threshold is breached so we can take preventative action.*

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Financial:** Increase in daily gross margin (measured in INR/Lakhs).
- **Operational:** 100% avoidance of unplanned rotating equipment failure.
- **Safety:** Zero pipeline freezing incidents.
- **Environmental:** 0% exceedance of monthly ESG flaring quotas.
